ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

The 2026 International Conference on Innovations in Emerging Technologies for Sustainable Development (ICIETSD) scheduled on APRIL 9-10,2026aims to brings together researchers, academicians, and industry experts to share ideas and innovations in sustainable and emerging technologies across sectors including computing, energy, infrastructure, environment, and security to drive a more sustainable future.

WHAT IS OUR GOAL?

Our goal is to promote research and innovation in cutting-edge and sustainable technologies that address global challenges across sectors like energy,environment,digital infrastructure and security.

  • Encourage innovations in areas like AI,Block Chain,IoT and 5G.
  • Support sustainable solutions in infrastructure,energy and manufacturing.
  • Foster eco-friendly and low-carbon technology development.
  • Advance cybersecurity,digitallisation and smart systems.
  • Enable collaboration between academia,industry and research communities.

Submit your original search work under the following innovative and sustainable technology domains(Not limited):
